Pega Course

Pega (Pegasystems) is a powerful enterprise software platform used for building business applications, especially for Customer Relationship Management (CRM), Business Process Management (BPM), and Digital Process Automation (DPA).The platform is known for its low-code/no-code environment, allowing users to create and manage applications with minimal coding expertise.
