Devops Course

DevOps integrates software development and IT operations to streamline processes, improve collaboration, and deliver software faster. Key practices include Continuous Integration/Continuous Delivery (CI/CD), automation, and monitoring. DevOps enhances agility, ensures high-quality releases, and improves operational efficiency.
